Need help?

Support

Do I need to create an account?

No. Online Booth works instantly in your browser with no sign-up, no login, and no account required.

Are my photos saved or uploaded anywhere?

No. Your photos are processed on your device. Nothing is uploaded to a server, and refreshing or closing the tab clears the session.

Which browsers are supported?

Online Booth works in modern browsers that support camera access, including Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari. It works best on desktop or laptop screens.

Why is my camera not showing up?

Your browser needs camera permission. Click the camera icon in the address bar, choose Allow, then reload. Also close apps that may already be using the camera.

Can I use Online Booth on mobile?

Yes, it works on modern mobile browsers, but the editing experience is smoother on a wider desktop or laptop screen.

How do I download my photo strip?

After your shots are taken and edited, open the result screen and click Download PNG to save the final strip to your device.

Can I change the layout mid-session?

Not while capturing. Choose your layout and timer before starting. To change them, retake the session and pick a new setup.

Is Online Booth free?

Yes. Online Booth is free to use, with no watermarks, hidden tiers, or paid downloads.
